Moisturizer

(n.) a lotion or cream used to prevent dryness in the skin.

Any creams, oils, lotions that you apply on your skin to make it less dry or keep it hydrated that is a moisturizer.

Sebum secreted from the body naturally that keeps your skin hydrated. When your skin lack sebum, Moisturizer is an external help to keep your skin hydrated.

Creams and lotions are cosmetic preparations used for protecting, moisturizing and lubricating skin. Suitable moisturizer will help to prevent dryness in the skin. It will shield sensitive skin, improve skin tone and texture, and mask imperfection.

Moisturizers also have ingredients to help nourish the skin with antioxidants, minor sunscreen protection, and other anti-aging benefits. The benefits of moisturizing your face every day will help you to keep the skin clear, glowing skin, softer, smoother, wrinkle-free and reduce the dry spots.
